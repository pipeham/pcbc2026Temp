The Cove Academy

A safe, Christ-centered homeschool alternative for preschool through grade 12, using the proven Accelerated Christian Education (A.C.E.) curriculum.

Our Mission & Curriculum

Founded in 2022, The Cove Academy provides a Biblically anchored learning environment where children grow academically and spiritually. We use the Accelerated Christian Education (A.C.E.) curriculum — a self-paced, mastery-based program recognized for over 35 years and accepted by universities.

Students work through colorful PACE booklets that integrate Scripture and character building into every subject.
