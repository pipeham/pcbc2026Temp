What We Believe

The Bible

Complete, inspired, inerrant Word of God — our final authority for faith and life. (2 Tim 3:16-17)

The Triune God

One God eternally existing in three Persons: Father, Son (Jesus Christ — fully God and fully man), and Holy Spirit.

Salvation & The Church

Salvation by grace through faith in Christ's substitutionary death and resurrection — preserved eternally. The local church is a body of baptized believers under Christ's lordship.

We affirm the core evangelical Baptist doctrines. A full doctrinal statement is available upon request or in our church constitution.
